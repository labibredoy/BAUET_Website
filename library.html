Library Rules & Regulations

Membership

All students and faculty members are automatically members. Valid ID card required for entry and borrowing.

Borrowing Rules

Students can borrow up to 3 books for 14 days. Faculty members can borrow up to 5 books for 30 days.

Fines & Penalties

Late return fine: 5 BDT per day per book. Lost book replacement cost plus processing fee.

General Rules

Maintain silence, no food or drinks, handle books with care, and follow staff instructions.
